How does this manifest?
pnyaj:
"If someone would to ask me “What is Hmong?” I can tell them what are Hmong and the background and history. But if the question “Who is the Hmong?” I can say my mom, dad, sisters, brothers, and anyone that is related to me."
Maintainance of kinship relations that are globally dispersed through the internet
young Australian Hmong find partners of different clans through instant messaging, chatrooms, fourms and email
rather than customary New Years Eve visits to other Hmong communities to find partners outsides one's own village.
